Who We Are

ResQChain is a revolutionary platform dedicated to ensuring fair and transparent distribution of essential goods during disasters.

Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge blockchain technology to create an immutable, transparent record of every transaction in the supply chain. This ensures that food, medicine, and other critical resources reach those who need them most, without corruption or mismanagement.

In times of crisis, transparency and accountability are essential. By providing real-time visibility, we empower communities, aid organizations, and governments to work together more effectively.

How It Works

1

Track

2

3

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform.

ResQChain is a blockchain-based platform that monitors and tracks the distribution of essential goods during disasters and emergencies. It ensures transparency, accountability, and fair distribution of resources like food, medicine, and fuel to those who need them most.

Blockchain creates an immutable, tamper-proof record of every transaction in the supply chain. Every movement of goods is recorded on the blockchain, making it impossible to alter or delete records. This ensures complete transparency and accountability throughout the distribution process.

The system is accessible to authorized stakeholders including government agencies, aid organizations, shelter operators, and verified civilians. Different user types have different levels of access based on their role in the distribution network.
